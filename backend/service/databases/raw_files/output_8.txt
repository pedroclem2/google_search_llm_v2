#  Beef Wellington

Beef Wellington is another excuse to show off your best reds and the rich and
varied flavours will stand-up well to a robust, slightly spicy wine. Try a
black fruit forward and peppery syrah, ripe Aussie shiraz or a cabernet-
dominated claret.
